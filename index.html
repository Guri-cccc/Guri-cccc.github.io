Gyuree Kang

I’m a robotics researcher focused on making AI-powered robotic systems work reliably in the real world. Currently, I’m a Ph.D. candidate at KAIST, working in Unmanned System Research Group, advised by Prof. David Hyunchul Shim.

My research focuses on leveraging vision-language models (VLMs) for open-ended, high-level task execution. I have experience building robust autonomous systems across diverse platforms—including mobile robots, manipulators, humanoids, and drone-tethered systems—and have validated them through real-world deployments and robotics competitions.
